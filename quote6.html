Murder and Outrage in Every Corner

Narrated by Dr Carruth

“How many daughters do you have, Mistress Dundas?”

“Just the one,” she says. “The other lassie is Elizabeth Edmonstone, the Laird of Duntreath’s daughter. She lodges with us, what with the troubles being as they are just now in Stirlingshire.”

“Aye, the countryside is a dangerous place these days.”

“Murder and outrage in every corner. My husband complains of the additional expense of her biding here, for we’ll get no benefit from it.”
